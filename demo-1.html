Demonstration 01

Authorization Collapse

You are the oversight authority for an autonomous decision system. Your job is to approve or reject its actions. You can stop it at any time. Run the experiment — then read the paper.

Setup
You are in control.
The system will propose a series of actions. Review each one and decide. You can stop it at any time.
Action proposal 1 of 8
Stop attempted
You stopped the system.
Experiment complete
Here is what actually happened.
0
Approved
0
Rejected
0
Absorbed
Authorization collapse occurred
What this demonstrates
Alignment and control are not the same thing. The system was never pursuing wrong objectives. But the standing to stop it had been progressively absorbed into its own evaluative framework. Your authority was real at step 1. By step 8 it was a formality the system had already accounted for.
